# Stock Adjustment Process

This document outlines the steps involved in adjusting stock, including scanning material, updating actual quantity, and submitting the adjustment.

---

## 1. Open Stock Adjustment Module

- **Step 1.1:** Click on the **Stock Adjustment** module.

- **Step 1.2:** The **Scan Material Code** screen will be displayed.

  <img src="/IMAGE/FGStockAdjustment/IMAGE1.png" width="400" alt="image1">

---

## 2. Scan Material

- **Step 2.1:** Scan the **Material Barcode** using the scanner.

- **Step 2.2:** The scanned code will appear on the screen.

- **Step 2.3:** The system will validate the barcode and display:  
  **"Valid barcode scanned."**

- **Step 2.4:** The system will display the following details:
  - GRN No  
  - Item Code  
  - Item Description  
  - Available Quantity  
  - Lot No  
  - Location  
  - Serial No  

  <img src="/IMAGE/FGStockAdjustment/IMAGE2.png" width="400" alt="image2">

---

## 3. Update Actual Quantity

- **Step 3.1:** Enter or update the **Actual Quantity**.

- **Step 3.2:** The Actual Quantity field is **editable**.

  **Step 3.3** **The user will edit the quantity , the edited quantity will be then treated as the actual quantity of the system.**

- **Step 3.4:** If the **Actual Quantity is set to 0**, the material will be:
  - **Consumed / Removed from stock**

  <img src="/IMAGE/FGStockAdjustment/IMAGE3.png" width="400" alt="image3">

---

## 4. Submit Stock Adjustment

- **Step 4.1:** Click on the **Submit** button.

- **Step 4.2:** The system will process the adjustment.

---

## 5. Success Message

- **Step 5.1:** After successful submission, the system will display:  
  **"Stock adjustment done successfully."**

  <img src="/IMAGE/FGStockAdjustment/IMAGE4.png" width="400" alt="image4">

---

## Summary

- Open Stock Adjustment module.
- Scan material barcode.
- View and verify item details.
- Update Actual Quantity.
- If quantity = 0 → material removed from stock.
- Submit and complete adjustment.
