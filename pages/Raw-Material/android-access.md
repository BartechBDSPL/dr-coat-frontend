# Android Device Registration & Approval Process

This document outlines the steps involved when a new user logs in from an unregistered Android device, followed by the device registration process, access verification, and final approval via swipe gesture.

---

## 1. Login Attempt from Unregistered Device

When a new user logs in from an unregistered device, the application will detect the device status and prompt for registration.

- **Step 1.1:** Enter your **Username** and **Password** in the login form.  

- **Step 1.2:** Click the **Login** button.

- **Step 1.3:** If the device is **not yet registered**, the user will be **redirected** to the **Device Registration** screen automatically.

The user needs to fill out the device registration form.

- **Step 2.1:** Enter the following details in the registration form:
  -

- **Step 2.2:** Click on the **Register** button to submit the registration request.

- **Step 2.3:** Once registered, the device details will be reflected in the **Web Portal** under the **Android Access Menu** section for administrative approval.

---

## 3. Web Portal: Android Access Menu

This section is used by the admin to manage registered Android devices.

- **Step 3.1:** Go to the **Android Access** section on the Web Portal.

- **Step 3.2:** The newly registered device will appear in the list.

- **Step 3.3:** Click the **Select** button next to the device to view and auto-fill its details in the input fields below.

  <img src="/IMAGE_RM/AndroidAccess/IMAGE4.png" width="400" alt="image5">

---

## 4. Swipe to Approve the Device

After selecting the device, it needs to be approved for access.

- **Step 4.1:** Swipe the **approval slider** to change the device’s status from **Pending** to **Approved**.

- **Step 4.2:** Once approved, the device can now access the Android application functionalities as per the assigned permissions.
