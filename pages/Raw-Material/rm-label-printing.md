# RM Label Printing Process

This document outlines the steps involved in generating and printing RM (Raw Material) labels, including GRN selection, label generation, and final printing confirmation.

---

## 1. Select GRN Number

The user needs to select the GRN number to fetch the details.

- **Step 1.1:** Navigate to the **RM Label Printing** screen.

- **Step 1.2:** Select or enter the **GRN No** from the dropdown list.

- **Step 1.3:** Click on the **Get Details** button.

  <img src="/IMAGE_RM/RMLabelPrinting/IMAGE7.png" width="400" alt="image1">

---

## 2. View GRN Details

After selecting the GRN number, the system will display all related details.

- **Step 2.1:** Once you click **Get Details**, the following information will be displayed:
  - Vendor Details  
  - PO Number & Date  
  - Item Code & Description  
  - Lot Number  
  - Quantity & Location  

- **Step 2.2:** Select the required line item for label printing by clicking on **Select**.

  <img src="/IMAGE_RM/RMLabelPrinting/IMAGE1.png" width="400" alt="image2">

---

## 3. Label Generation

Enter the required weight details to generate labels.

- **Step 3.1:** In the **Label Generation** section, enter:
  - **Base Weight (KGS)**  
  - **Tare Weight (KGS)**  

- **Step 3.2:** The **Total Weight (KGS)** will be **calculated automatically**.

- **Step 3.3:** Enter the **Total Quantity** if required.
   **Note:- Suppose the total quantity of Production Order is 1000 and user want to print the label of 500 quantity only then he will edit the total quantity field to 500.**

- **Step 3.4:** Click on **Generate Serial Numbers**.

  <img src="/IMAGE_RM/RMLabelPrinting/IMAGE3.png" width="400" alt="image3">

---

## 4. Enter Date & Printer Details

Provide manufacturing and printing details.

- **Step 4.1:** Select the **Manufacturing Date**.

- **Step 4.2:** The **Expiry Date** will be **auto-calculated (1 year increment)**.

- **Step 4.3:** Select the **Printer** from the dropdown.

  <img src="/IMAGE_RM/RMLabelPrinting/IMAGE4.png" width="400" alt="image4">

---

## 5. Print Labels

Proceed to print the generated labels.

- **Step 5.1:** Click on the **Print Labels** button.

---

## 6. Confirm Before Printing

A confirmation popup will appear before final printing.

- **Step 6.1:** Verify the following details:
  - Base Weight  
  - Tare Weight  
  - Total Weight  
  - Serial Numbers  
  - GRN Details  
  - Printer Name  

- **Step 6.2:** Click on the **Print** button to proceed.

  <img src="/IMAGE_RM/RMLabelPrinting/IMAGE5.png" width="400" alt="image6">

---

## 7. Success Message

- After successful printing, the system will display a confirmation message:  
  **"Inserted and printed labels successfully."**
