# Material Consumption Process

This document outlines the steps involved in consuming raw materials using the Material Consumption module.

---

## 1. Open Material Consumption Module

- **Step 1.1:** Click on the **Material Consumption** module.

- **Step 1.2:** The **Material Consumption** screen will be displayed.

  <img src="/IMAGE_RM/RMMaterialConsumption/IMAGE1.png" width="400" alt="image1">

---

## 2. Scan Material

- **Step 2.1:** Scan the material using the **Scan Material Code** field.

- **Step 2.2:** The scanned code will appear on the screen.

- **Step 2.3:** The system will validate the barcode and display the message:  
  **"Valid barcode scanned."**

- **Step 2.4:** The system will display the following details:
  - Item Code  
  - Item Description  
  - Quantity  
  - Lot Number  
  - Warehouse Code  
  - Location  
  - Serial Number  

  <img src="/IMAGE_RM/RMMaterialConsumption/IMAGE2.png" width="400" alt="image2">

---

## 3. Consume Material

- **Step 3.1:** Click on the **Consume** button.

  <img src="/IMAGE_RM/RMMaterialConsumption/IMAGE3.png" width="400" alt="image3">

---

## 4. Success Message

- **Step 4.1:** After consuming the material, the system will display a success message:  
  **"Material consumed successfully."**

  <img src="/IMAGE_RM/RMMaterialConsumption/IMAGE4.png" width="400" alt="image4">

---

## 5. Important Note

- **After consuming the material, the item quantity will be reduced from RM inventory and the same material cannot be scanned or used in any other module.**
