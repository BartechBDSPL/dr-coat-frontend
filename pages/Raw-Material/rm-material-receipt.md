# Raw Material Receipt Process

This document outlines the steps involved in receiving raw materials using the Stock Transfer Receipt module, including selecting transfer orders, scanning materials, and completing the receipt process.

---

## 1. Open Raw Material Receipt Module

The user needs to access the Raw Material Receipt screen.

- **Step 1.1:** Navigate to the **Raw Material Receipt** module.

- **Step 1.2:** The **Raw Material Receipt** screen will be displayed.

  <img src="/IMAGE_RM/RMMaterialReceipt/IMAGE1.png" width="400" alt="image1">

---

## 2. Select Stock Transfer Number

Select the stock transfer order for receiving materials.

- **Step 2.1:** Click on the **Stock Transfer No** dropdown.

- **Step 2.2:** The system will display available stock transfer numbers.

- **Step 2.3:** Select the required **Stock Transfer No**.

- **Step 2.4:** The selected transfer number will be displayed on the screen.

  <img src="/IMAGE_RM/RMMaterialReceipt/IMAGE2.png" width="400" alt="image2">

---

## 3. Scan Material

Scan the material barcode to receive items.

- **Step 3.1:** Click on the **Scan Material Code** field.

- **Step 3.2:** Scan the barcode using the scanner.

- **Step 3.3:** The scanned code will appear on the screen.

- **Step 3.4:** The system will validate the scanned material.

 

---

## 4. Validation Message

System validates whether the scanned material is correct.

### 4.1 Valid Case

- **Step 4.1.1:** If the material is valid, it will be marked as received.

- **Step 4.1.2:** The material will be removed from the pending list.

- **Step 4.1.3:** Material count will be updated automatically.

---

### 4.2 Invalid Case

- **Step 4.2.1:** If the material is not picked against the selected transfer, an error message will be displayed:  
  **"Material is not picked against this stock transfer."**

   <img src="/IMAGE_RM/RMMaterialReceipt/IMAGE3.png" width="400" alt="image3">

 

---

## 5. Complete Receipt

After scanning all materials, the receipt process will be completed.

- **Step 5.1:** Once all materials are scanned, the system will display a success message:  
  **"Receipt completed for Stock Transfer No: TRO/SHSP/00001. Do the PUT AWAY."**

- **Step 5.2:** The stock transfer will be marked as completed.

- **Step 5.3:** User can proceed with the **Put Away** process.
