# Stock Take Process

This document outlines the steps involved in performing stock take, including initiating a new stock take, selecting warehouse & location, scanning materials, and submitting the count.

---

## 1. Initiate Stock Take

- **Step 1.1:** Click on the **Stock Take** module from RM Management.

- **Step 1.2:** If it is the **first time**, a popup will appear:
  **"Initiate New Stock Take"**

  **Step 1.3:** If you have started the stock take and want to **continue again**, a popup will appear:
  **"Start New or Continue"** , click on the continue.

- **Step 1.4:** Click on **Start New Stock Take**.

  <img src="/IMAGE_RM/RMStockTake/IMAGE1.png" width="400" alt="image1">

---

## 2. Open Stock Take Screen

- **Step 2.1:** After clicking **Start New Stock Take**, the main **Stock Take screen** will be displayed.

  <img src="/IMAGE_RM/RMStockTake/IMAGE2.png" width="400" alt="image2">

---

## 3. Select Warehouse

- **Step 3.1:** Select the **Warehouse Code** from the dropdown.

- **Step 3.2:** The selected warehouse will be displayed.

  <img src="/IMAGE_RM/RMStockTake/IMAGE3.png" width="400" alt="image3">

---

## 4. Select Location

- **Step 4.1:** Click on **Scan Location Code** or use suggestion icon.

- **Step 4.2:** The **Location Suggestion** screen will appear.

- **Step 4.3:** **Double-click** on the required location (Bin).

- **Step 4.4:** Selected location will be displayed on the main screen.

  <img src="/IMAGE_RM/RMStockTake/IMAGE4.png" width="400" alt="image4">

---

## 5. Scan Material

- **Step 5.1:** Scan the **Material Barcode**.

- **Step 5.2:** The scanned code will appear on the screen.

---

### 5.1 Location Mismatch Case

- If the scanned material belongs to a **different location**, the system will display an orange warning message:

  **"The current location of this item in the system is: [Location]"**

  <img src="/IMAGE_RM/RMStockTake/IMAGE5.png" width="400" alt="image5">

---

## 6. Verify Quantity

- **Step 6.1:** After scanning, the system will display:
  - GRN No  
  - Item Code  
  - Item Description  
  - Lot No  
  - Serial No  
  - System Location  
  - **System Quantity**  
  - **Physical Quantity**

- **Step 6.2:** The **Physical Quantity** field is **editable** if in case user wants to edit the quantity.

  <img src="/IMAGE_RM/RMStockTake/IMAGE6.png" width="400" alt="image6">

---

## 7. Submit Stock Take

- **Step 7.1:** Click on the **Submit** button.

- **Step 7.2:** The system will display success message:  
  **"Stock Take done successfully."**

  <img src="/IMAGE_RM/RMStockTake/IMAGE7.png" width="400" alt="image7">

---

## 8. Duplicate Scan Validation

- **Step 8.1:** If the same material is scanned again in the same stock take:

- **Step 8.2:** The system will display an error message:  
  **"Item is already scanned against this stock take no."**

  <img src="/IMAGE_RM/RMStockTake/IMAGE8.png" width="400" alt="image8">

---

## Summary

- Initiate new stock take (first time popup).
- Select warehouse and location.
- Scan material barcode.
- Handle location mismatch warning if applicable.
- Verify and edit physical quantity.
- Submit stock take.
- System prevents duplicate scanning.
