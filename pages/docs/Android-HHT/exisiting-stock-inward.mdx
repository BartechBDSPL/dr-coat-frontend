# Existing Stock Inward Process Documentation

This document outlines the step-by-step process for performing stock inward operations using the Existing Stock Inward module. It details the procedure of warehouse and bin selection, barcode scanning, quantity confirmation, and stock update.

---

## 1. Open the Stock Inward Module

- Navigate to the **Existing Stock Inward** module in the application.
- This module is used for recording stock entries into specific warehouse bins.

![image1](/DOCS_IMAGE/existing-stock-inward/Image1.jpeg)

---

## 2. Select the Warehouse

- **Step 2.1:** Select the appropriate **Warehouse Name** where you intend to inward the stock.

![image2](/DOCS_IMAGE/existing-stock-inward/Image2.jpeg)



---

## 3. Scan Bin Number

- **Step 3.1:** Scan the **Bin Number** using the scanner.
- **Validation Check:**
  - If the scanned bin does **not exist** in the selected warehouse, an **error message** will be displayed:
    > ⚠️ **Bin not found in Location Master**

![image3](/DOCS_IMAGE/existing-stock-inward/Image3.jpeg)

---

## 4. Handle Invalid Barcode or Bin Scan
  - popup will open.
  - Double-click on any location to select it.

![image5](/DOCS_IMAGE/existing-stock-inward/Image4.jpeg)

---

## 5. Confirm Valid Location

- **Step 5.1:** Once a valid location is selected (via scanning or from suggestions), you will receive a message:
  > ✅ **Valid Location**

![image6](/DOCS_IMAGE/existing-stock-inward/Image5.jpeg)

---

## 6. Scan Pallet Code

- **Step 6.1:** After confirming the location, scan the **Pallet Code**.
- **Step 6.2:** All relevant **details will be fetched from the database** and displayed automatically.

![image7](/DOCS_IMAGE/existing-stock-inward/Image6.jpeg)

---

## 7. Modify Confirm Quantity (Optional)

- **Step 7.1:** You may change the **Confirm Quantity** field if required.
- The system will automatically **recalculate the Number of Quantities** based on the new confirmation.

![image8](/DOCS_IMAGE/existing-stock-inward/Image7.jpeg)

---

## 8. Handle Duplicate Barcode Scan

- **Step 8.1:** If you **scan the barcode again** for the same entry:
  - The system will automatically set:
    > Confirm Quantity = Quantity

![image9](/DOCS_IMAGE/existing-stock-inward/Image8.jpeg).


---

## 9. Finalize Stock Update

- **Step 9.1:** Click the **"UP" button** to finalize the stock inward entry.
- **Step 9.2:** Upon successful update, you will receive the message:
  > ✅ **Successfully Stock Update**

![image10](/DOCS_IMAGE/existing-stock-inward/Image9.jpeg)

---
