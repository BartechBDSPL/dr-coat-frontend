# FG Material Movement Process Documentation

This document outlines the detailed steps for the FG (Finished Goods) Material Movement process, including Internal Movement operations. It provides a comprehensive guide for scanning locations, validating materials, handling errors, and transferring goods.

---

## 1. FG Material Movement Menu

- Upon login, the **FG Material Movement** menu is displayed.
- Navigate to the **Internal Movement** option.
  
  <img src="/DOCS_IMAGE/hht-Internal-Transfer/image0.jpeg" alt="FG Material Movement Menu" width="250px"/>

---

## 2. Internal Movement Page

- Click on **Internal Movement** to open the **Internal Movement Page**.

  <img src="/DOCS_IMAGE/hht-Internal-Transfer/image1.jpeg" alt="FG Material Movement Menu" width="250px"/>

---

## 3. Scanning Pick Location

### **Step 3.1:** Use the scanner to scan the **Pick Location** in the `Scan Pick Location` field.
### **Step 3.2:** The system validates the scanned location:
  - **Valid Location:** Displays the message "**Location valid**". The field gets disabled, and the cursor moves to the `Scan Material` field.

  <img src="/DOCS_IMAGE/hht-Internal-Transfer/image2.jpeg" alt="FG Material Movement Menu" width="250px"/>

  - **Invalid Location:** Displays the error message "**Invalid location scan. Not found in WH location master**".

  <img src="/DOCS_IMAGE/hht-Internal-Transfer/image3.jpeg" alt="FG Material Movement Menu" width="250px"/>

### **Step 3.3:** If needed, click the **Refresh Button** near the `Scan Pick Location` field to clear the input and scan a new location.

---

## 4. Scanning Material

### **Step 4.1:** After validating the Pick Location, scan the **Box Number** or **Pallet Number** in the `Scan Material` field using the scanner.
### **Step 4.2:** The system performs validations:

  - **Pending Put Away Status:**
    - If the **Put Away** status is pending, the system prevents the process and displays the message:
      > "**Scan Pallet Barcode Material Put Away are pending, not able to do the internal transfer.**"

      <img src="/DOCS_IMAGE/hht-Internal-Transfer/image8.jpeg" alt="FG Material Movement Menu" width="250px"/>

  - **Valid Box Number:**
    - Displays the message "**Scanned Material Successfully!**" in green.
    - Hidden fields are displayed automatically, showing data related to the scanned box.
    - Relevant fields such as **Order Number**, **Batch**, **Material**, **Material Description**, **Quantity**, **Production Plant** are populated.
    - The cursor focuses on the `Put Location` field.

      <img src="/DOCS_IMAGE/hht-Internal-Transfer/image5.jpeg" alt="FG Material Movement Menu" width="250px"/>

  - **Invalid Box Number:**
    - Displays the error message:
      > "**Invalid Barcode Scan. Not Found in the FG Label Printing..!**"

      <img src="/DOCS_IMAGE/hht-Internal-Transfer/image9.jpeg" alt="FG Material Movement Menu" width="250px"/>

  - **Duplicate Scan:**
    - If the box number is scanned more than once, the system shows:
      > "**Duplicate - Serial Number already scanned.**"

      <img src="/DOCS_IMAGE/hht-Internal-Transfer/image6.jpeg" alt="FG Material Movement Menu" width="250px"/>

  - **Different Location:**
    - If the material is located at a different location, the system displays:
      > "**Scan Pallet Barcode is available on another location, not able to transfer. Current location: [Location Name]**"

      <img src="/DOCS_IMAGE/hht-Internal-Transfer/image4.jpeg" alt="FG Material Movement Menu" width="250px"/>

---

## 5. Removing Scanned Serial Number

- To remove a scanned serial number:
  - Check the checkbox for the serial number.
  - Scan the serial number again.
  - The system displays the message in blue:
    > "**Serial number is removed.**"

    <img src="/DOCS_IMAGE/hht-Internal-Transfer/image7.jpeg" alt="FG Material Movement Menu" width="250px"/>

---

## 6. Scanning or Selecting Put Location

### **Step 6.1:** Use the scanner to scan the `Put Location` or click on the **Location Icon** near the field to open the **Location Suggestion Page**.
  
  - **Location Suggestion Page:**
    - Displays a table with available locations.
    - Double-tap on the desired location to select it.
    - The unique location code is displayed in the `Put Location` field.

       <img src="/DOCS_IMAGE/hht-Internal-Transfer/image10.jpeg" alt="FG Material Movement Menu" width="250px"/>

### **Step 6.2:** The system validates the `Put Location`:

  - **Valid Location:**
    - Displays the message "**Valid location**".

       <img src="/DOCS_IMAGE/hht-Internal-Transfer/image11.jpeg" alt="FG Material Movement Menu" width="250px"/>

  - **Pick Location Same as put Location:**
    - Displays the message:
      > "**Location cannot be the same.**"

      <img src="/DOCS_IMAGE/hht-Internal-Transfer/image12.jpeg" alt="FG Material Movement Menu" width="250px"/>

  - **Blank Location:**
    - If no location is selected and click on Transfer Button, displays the error:
      > "**Pick Location cannot be blank.**"

      <img src="/DOCS_IMAGE/hht-Internal-Transfer/image13.jpeg" alt="FG Material Movement Menu" width="250px"/>

---

## 7. Completing the Transfer

### **Step 7.1:** After validating all data, click the **Transfer** button.

   <img src="/DOCS_IMAGE/hht-Internal-Transfer/image14.jpeg" alt="FG Material Movement Menu" width="250px"/>

### **Step 7.2:** The system processes the transfer and displays the success message:
  > "**Transfer Successful!**"

  <img src="/DOCS_IMAGE/hht-Internal-Transfer/image15.jpeg" alt="FG Material Movement Menu" width="250px"/>

---


This concludes the detailed process for the Internal Movement operation. For further assistance, contact the support team.
