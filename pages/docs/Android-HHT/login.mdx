# Warehouse Management System (WMS) Login and Configuration Process

This document outlines the steps for logging in and configuring the Warehouse Management System (WMS). It includes the process for entering credentials, configuring network settings, and navigating to the main menu.

---

## 1. Open the Application

- **Step 1.1:** Launch the WMS application.  
  The login page will appear as the starting interface. 

  <img src="/DOCS_IMAGE/hht-login/image0.jpeg" alt="FG Material Movement Menu" width="250px" />

---

## 2. Enter Login Credentials

- **Step 2.1:** On the login page, enter your **Username** and **Password** in the respective fields.  

  <img src="/DOCS_IMAGE/hht-login/image1.jpeg" alt="FG Material Movement Menu" width="250px" />

- **Step 2.2:** Click the **Login** button.  
  A message prompting you to "Please Select Network" will appear.  

  <img src="/DOCS_IMAGE/hht-login/image3.jpeg" alt="FG Material Movement Menu" width="250px" />

---

## 3. Access Settings for Network Configuration

- **Step 3.1:** Locate the **Settings Icon** in the top-right corner of the login page.  

  <img src="/DOCS_IMAGE/hht-login/image4.jpeg" alt="FG Material Movement Menu" width="250px" />


- **Step 3.2:** Click on the **Settings Icon** to navigate to the **Web Service Configuration** page. 
                
     On the **Web Service Configuration** page, fill in the following fields:  
       - **Web Tata**  
       - **Web Other**  
       - **Web LAN**  
       - **Port** (Mandatory)

    <img src="/DOCS_IMAGE/hht-login/image6.jpeg" alt="FG Material Movement Menu" width="250px" />

---

## 4. Configure Network and Port Settings

- **Step 4.1:** Ensure the following:  
  - At least one of the **Web Fields** (Web Tata, Web Other, or Web LAN) is filled.  
  - The **Port Number** is mandatory and must be valid.  

    <img src="/DOCS_IMAGE/hht-login/image7.jpeg" alt="FG Material Movement Menu" width="250px" />

- **Step 4.2:** Click the **Save** button to save the IP address and port number configurations.  

   <img src="/DOCS_IMAGE/hht-login/image8.jpeg" alt="FG Material Movement Menu" width="250px" />

  **Note:** Ensure that the IP addresses and port numbers are valid and accessible for successful connectivity.  

- **Step 4.3:** After saving, you will be navigated back to the **Login Page**.  
  
---

## 5. Select Network on the Login Page

- **Step 5.1:** On the login page, a **Network Selector Spinner** will appear at the bottom of the screen.  
 
- **Step 5.2:** Choose the appropriate network configuration from the drop-down menu.  

   <img src="/DOCS_IMAGE/hht-login/image10.jpeg" alt="FG Material Movement Menu" width="250px" />

  **Note:** Ensure that you select the correct network before proceeding.  

---

## 4. Login Authentication

- **Step 4.1:** Click the **Login** button to authenticate.

### Login Success:
- Upon successful authentication, the **Main Menu Page** will be displayed.

  <img src="/DOCS_IMAGE/hht-login/image11.jpeg" alt="FG Material Movement Menu" width="250px" />

### Login Errors:
- **Incorrect Password:** If an incorrect password is entered, the system displays the message:
  - `Invalid Credentials. Please try again.`

  <img src="/DOCS_IMAGE/hht-login/image13.jpeg" alt="FG Material Movement Menu" width="250px" />

  
- **Account Lock:** After three failed login attempts, the system locks the user account and displays:
  - `Your account is locked due to too many failed login attempts. Please contact the administrator.`

  <img src="/DOCS_IMAGE/hht-login/image14.jpeg" alt="FG Material Movement Menu" width="250px" />


---

This concludes the detailed process for logging in and configuring the Warehouse Management System (WMS). If you encounter any issues, please contact the support team.
